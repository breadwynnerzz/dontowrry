## Claims Still Treated As Estimates

- Drive times are approximate planning ranges and can change sharply with traffic, weather, parking, event timing, and July 4th travel congestion.
- Budget ranges are rough 2026 planning estimates for a 2027 trip, not quotes. The current displayed ranges are budget-guarded and assume shared lodging, selective attraction days, limited extras, and active deal hunting.
- Attraction ticket prices, resort rates, parking, taxes, service fees, and hours are not final for July 2027.
- Itineraries are sample planning sequences and should be rebuilt around final park hours, flight times, reservation windows, and family energy.
- Airport convenience depends on actual flights from North Carolina, rental car pricing, and arrival/departure times.

## July 2027 Uncertainty

- The trip dates are more than a year away from this fact-check date.
- Many official 2027 calendars, summer ticket prices, park hours, fireworks schedules, event schedules, resort fees, parking prices, and holiday-week availability are not published yet.
- Before booking, the family should verify each attraction directly on its official website and re-check lodging and airfare prices for July 2-7, 2027.
